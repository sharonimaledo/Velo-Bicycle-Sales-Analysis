# Project Brief: Velo Bicycle Sales Performance and Seasonality Analysis
## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze multi-year bicycle sales data to uncover trends in revenue performance, product behavior, customer demand, and seasonality, and to translate these findings into actionable business insights. The analysis was conducted as part of a Business Intelligence Analyst technical assessment and is designed to demonstrate analytical reasoning, data transformation skills, and insight communication.
## Context
Velo operates across multiple markets and sells a diverse range of bicycle products and accessories. Despite having a large volume of transactional data, the business requires deeper insight into:

•	How sales performance differs across countries over time

•	Which products and categories drive or hinder revenue growth

•	Whether expected seasonal purchasing patterns hold across all products

•	How major economic events, such as the 2008 Lehman Brothers financial crisis, influenced sales behavior

Stakeholders are particularly interested in understanding historical performance drivers to support strategic planning, product focus, and risk mitigation.
## Key Business Questions
Section A – Revenue Trends and Economic Impact

•	How has sales revenue evolved over time in the United States and Germany?

•	What factors in the data help explain the observed revenue behavior?

•	Excluding the customer Silicon Valley Bikes, how does monthly sales revenue compare year-over-year, and how do these trends relate to the 2008 financial crisis?

Section B – Product Performance and Seasonality

•	Which product recorded the lowest overall quantity sold each year?

•	Which product category generated the highest revenue each year?

•	For 2011:

o	What percentage of total bicycle sales quantity was contributed by Off-Road Bikes?

o	In which three cities was this contribution significantly above average?

•	Are there products that exhibit a lack of expected seasonality, despite typical industry purchasing patterns?

•	What insights and strategic recommendations can be derived from the analysis?
## Stakeholders
•	Business Leadership / Hiring Panel – Evaluating analytical depth, data reasoning, and insight quality

•	Sales and Strategy Teams – Interested in revenue drivers, underperforming products, and market behavior

•	Product Management – Seeking insight into product seasonality and lifecycle performance
## Data
The analysis uses a high-volume transactional sales dataset containing:

•	Sales revenue and quantity metrics

•	Product categories and descriptions

•	Customer and geographic (country and city) information

•	Time-based transaction records

All monetary values were validated and analyzed in U.S. currency.
## Analysis Criteria
•	Data Transformation: Performed using Power Query 

•	Modeling and Calculations: Conducted in Power BI using appropriate measures and time-based logic

•	Visualization: Interactive dashboards designed in Power BI to clearly answer each assessment question 

•	Business Relevance: Insights linked directly to economic context and commercial implications
## Project Deliverables
•	Power BI Report – Interactive dashboards addressing all questions in Sections A and B
•	Presentation – Executive-level summary of insights and recommendations
